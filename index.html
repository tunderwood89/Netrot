The End of the Internet

A website exploring the collapse of the World Wide Web.

Why is the web collapsing?

Artifical Intelligence

AI has caused many SEO-optimised AI generated websites to soar to the top of the search results on search engines like Google, causing the web to become less organic, while also causing journalists to lose their jobs.

Account Deletion

Google is mass-deleting old accounts older than 2 years, destroying crucial parts of history on YouTube and Google Drive, without making any backups. This causes link rot.

Centralization

The web is increasingly dominated by a few large companies, such as Google, Amazon, Meta, and Apple. This centralization can lead to a lack of competition, stifling innovation and leading to privacy concerns and monopolistic behaviors.

How can we stop this?

Promote Competition

We need to start using more websites like Mastodon or Peertube. Using these will make big tech companies realise that they need to make a better effort to make everything better for users.
